Fairygodmummy's Blueberry pie

Wanna have a bite??

Cinderella had a fairy godmother that was there to make her signature blueberry pie. Do you have one?

Worry not! Make one yourself!

INGREDIENTS

  • For the Dough

    • All-purpose flour
    • Shortening, butter, or lard
    • Cold water
    • Salt

  • For the Blueberry Filling

    • Blueberries
    • Sugar
    • Flour
    • Lemon juice and zest

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Make filling: Mix 3 cups blueberries, ½ cup sugar, 2 tbsp cornstarch, 1 tsp lemon juice, pinch of salt.
  3. Prepare pie crust: Line a pie dish with bottom crust.
  4. Add filling: Pour the blueberry mixture into the crust.
  5. Top crust: Cover with second crust (or lattice style), seal edges, and cut slits for steam.
  6. Brush top with egg wash or milk, sprinkle some sugar.
  7. Bake 40-45 mins, until golden and bubbling
  8. Cool completely before slicing (helps set the filling).
